I am an economist based in Vienna, Austria. I am currently working as a Senior Economist at the [Austrian Institute for Economic Research (WIFO)](https://www.wifo.ac.at), and as an Assistant Professor at the [Vienna University of Economics and Business (WU)](https://www.wu.ac.at/). Previously, I was a Research Fellow at the [International Institute for Applied System Analysis (IIASA)](https://iiasa.ac.at/) (2017-2020) and the Research Director at the [Center for Economic Research in Pakistan (CERP)](https://www.cerp.org.pk) (2011-2013).

I received my Ph.D. in Economics from the [New School for Social Research (New York)](https://www.newschool.edu/nssr/) (2007-2012) and my Habilitation in Economics from the WU in 2020. Prior to 2007, I was at the [Lahore University of Management Sciences (LUMS)](https://lums.edu.pk) (2004-2007) working on various research projects.

My current research work focuses on developing climate diagnostic tools to evaluate real-financial climate risks. I also work with big data, climate analytics, and developing data science tools. I blog on [Medium](https://medium.com/the-stata-guide) and I am active on [Twitter/X](https://twitter.com/AsjadNaqvi).
